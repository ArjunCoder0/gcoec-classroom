Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download a PDF?

Select your session, branch, semester, and subject, then click "Get Question Paper". Once the paper is found, click the "Download PDF" button.

What if my subject is not listed?

Contact us on WhatsApp with your subject details, and we'll try to add it to our database as soon as possible.

Are the PDFs free to download?

Yes! All previous year question papers are completely free to download and use for your studies.

How often are new papers added?

We add new papers after each exam session (Summer and Winter). Contact us if you need a specific paper urgently.

Response Time: We typically respond within 1-2 hours during business hours
