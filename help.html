Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my classes?

Login with your student credentials, then navigate to your dashboard to see all enrolled classes with notes and assignments.

How do I submit assignments?

Go to your class, click on the Assignments tab, select the assignment, and upload your file. Supported formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, ZIP, RAR (Max 10MB).

Are the classroom features free?

Yes! All GCOEC Classroom features including notes access, assignment submission, and grade tracking are completely free for students.

What if I can't login?

Contact us on WhatsApp with your student details. Make sure you're using the correct credentials provided by your college. Try clearing browser cache or using incognito mode.

How do I view my grades?

After submitting assignments, grades will appear in the same assignments section. You'll see your score, feedback from teachers, and submission status.
